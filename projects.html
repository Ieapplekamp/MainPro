Nomadic Security

Venture forth to anywhere in the world, while Nomadic Security gives you peace of mind. Plan a trip and have the security knowing that someone will make sure you’re back home in time. If you’re unresponsive or in trouble when we reach out, emergency services will come to your rescue.

For this project, my main focus was user authentication with Bcrytpt.js, Passport.js, and JWT. Once the authentication was established, I switched gears to deisgn for the landing and dashboard components. You'll find we used React-Bootstrap, CSS3, and Particles.js. For the background, I photoshopped the mountains into two separate photos and called the particles between the components, to give the sky and star-like effect.

G-Books!

This app utilizes the Google Books API and places them on this webpage. Once you retrieve the list, you can either "View Book" or "Bookmark" it. If you choose to view the book, it will redirect you to that book's landing page on Google Play. If you click on the bookmark, you can save multiple(different) books, and access them later in the "Saved Books" page. It will also add them to your mongoDB. In the Saved Books landing page, you can view the book in the Google Books website or you can delete it from your saved queue.
